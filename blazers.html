About BLAZERS

We are a passionate team of innovators and technology enthusiasts from Pan-Atlantic University, dedicated to advancing Nigeria's position in the global technology landscape. Our mission is to create groundbreaking solutions that will transform how Africa approaches space exploration, robotics, and emerging technologies.

Through our cutting-edge projects like the Mars Rover Prototype, we're not just building technology – we're building the future. Every project we undertake is a step toward making Nigeria a recognized hub for technological innovation and space exploration.
